🚀 3. How to Run for the Demo
Close any running terminals.
Double-click the start-all.bat file in the root D:\AI HACKATHON directory.
This will automatically launch the Python backend, the Node.js backend, and the Expo bundler.
Open the Expo Go app on your phone and scan the QR code.
📦 4. Generating the APK
I attempted to generate the APK for you, but Expo requires you to securely log into your account first. To generate the final .apk file for submission, open a new terminal in the mobile-app folder and run:

bash
npm install -g eas-cli
eas login
eas build -p android --profile preview
It will ask for your Expo email/password. After about 10 minutes, it will give you a direct download link to your .apk file.
